"ABOUT US"

We are a team of 5 like-minded members who have come together for creating a project titled "Hospital Management System". We are a group of students studying Computer Engineering at Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT), Pune, India. With a guided motivation due to the COVID-19 pandemic and Web Development being a common field of interest, we decided to choose "Hospital Management System" as the topic for our project.

"MOTIVATION"

During the COVID-19 pandemic, all of us were reminded that healthcare is the most critical aspect of our society, but many healthcare providers face challenges to offer practical and active services to patients. Considering a multi-speciality hospital, many people enter and exit the hospital in a day and maintaining their records safely is tedious. To reduce this burden and to manage the hospital administration and other clinical aspects, we thought of creating a "Hospital Management System."
